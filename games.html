  • Game 2

    The Zombies Mode returns in Black Ops 3 as a full-game experience, featuring its own storyline. An XP-based progression system has also been implemented that adds more depth and re-playability to the gameplay, The objective of the game is to survive each round of invading zombies after which a more difficult round begins,Zombies enter from outside the playable map via barricaded entries. As the rounds progress, difficulty increases and the zombies can remove barricades faster. Players are awarded points for replacing barricades as well as killing zombies.
